Hello, this is my mathtool.
It calculates the amount of gas you need and how much it is goingto cost to travel those kilometers.
I made this with Javascript for a school project it took me about 2 hours for the Javascript and about 4 hours for the CSS (that is a styling program).
The gas prices for the benelux are displayed at the right side of the screen and get updated once a month.
For the calculator to work you will need to know 2 things:
1. How many kilometers you want to drive.
2. How much kilometers your car can drive on one liter gasoline.
If you know all that you can fill it in and add the gasoline prices then the calculator will tel you how much it is going to cost in euros.
